Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Home

Description:

Who doesn't love chocolate? These chocolate crinkle cookies are so soft and packed with chocolate flavor and they're pretty fancy looking to boot. Crinkle cookies are a holiday tradition in my family, and once you try these, they're sure to be a new tradition for you too.

Disclaimer: This recipe contains eggs and products made in the same factory as nut-based products.

Ingredients:

Quantity: Makes 24 cookies

  • Vegetable Oil - 1/2 cup
  • Unsweetened baking chocolate - 4 oz (if you can find Dutch processed, that's even better!)
  • Sugar, granulated white - 2 cups
  • Vanilla extract - 2 tsp
  • Large eggs - 4 eggs
  • All Purpose (AP) flour - 2 cups
  • Baking Powder - 2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Powdered Sugar - 1/2 cup

Steps:

  1. Mix oil, granulated sugar, vanilla, and chocolate in a bowl
  2. Stir eggs in one at a time
  3. Stir in flour, baking powder, and salt
  4. Heat oven to 350°F
  5. Drop spoonfulls of cookie dough into powdered sugar until ball is covered
  6. Bake for 10-12 min

Serving:

Serve on a plate and get eating!
